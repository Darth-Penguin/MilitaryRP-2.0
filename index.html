What is MilitaryRP 2.0?

MilitaryRP 2.0 is a version of MRP that is more focused on role play. Basically, it's DarkRP, just even more military themed.

Why is this on GitHub?

Because I like to keep back up files in case I come across an coding accident. Good to keep back ups.

Can I use these files?

Sure. But you'll be banned from my server and anybody who comes across "your" server will spite you for copying my code.

This isn't the whole gamemode? Where is it?

I don't want anybody coming along and just making their own MilitaryRP 2.0. So I have only included certain files like the jobs file, and weapon shipments file because those are the files that are annoying to make.
